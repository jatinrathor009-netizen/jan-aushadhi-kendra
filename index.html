What is Jan Aushadhi Kendra?

Pradhan Mantri Jan Aushadhi Kendra is a retail outlet established under the Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Jan Aushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) scheme launched by the Government of India's Bureau of Pharma PSUs.

Our mission is to provide quality generic medicines at affordable prices to all citizens, especially the poor and deprived sections of society, reducing the out-of-pocket expenditure on medicines.
